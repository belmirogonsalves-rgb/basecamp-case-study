## S07 — SOLUTION: WEBSITE

**Section counter:** 05
**Section label:** The Solution

**Headline:**
Building a Website Worthy of Svalbard

**Intro:**
The brief was clear: sell the feeling of Svalbard, not a list of products. Immersive, lead-generating, and scalable — a platform that grows with the business.

---

### Design Philosophy Block
*(Playfair Display italic opening line. DM Sans body. Place before feature grid.)*

**Opening line (Playfair italic, large):**
Svalbard cannot be added to a cart.

**Body (3 paragraphs):**
The experience Basecamp Explorer sells — armed guides, glacier access, remote wilderness lodges — is not a product you browse and buy. It is a conversation. A decision that begins with awe and ends with a phone call.

The new site was built not to close a sale, but to earn one. Full-viewport imagery, minimal UI chrome, and narrative-led layouts were chosen deliberately — to immerse the visitor in the feeling of Svalbard before a single feature or price point appears.

Every page leads somewhere intentional. The call-to-action architecture is built around one conversion event: a qualified lead picking up the phone. This is not an e-commerce site. It is a digital expedition briefing.

---

### What We Built — Feature Display
**Background video:** `../assets/videos/the-solution.mp4` — autoplay, muted, loop, overlay `rgba(12,15,10,0.88)`

**Display style:** Interactive accordion. Each feature is a horizontal row. On hover/click it expands to reveal full description. One open at a time. Smooth height transition.

**8 features:**

1. **Video Hero Homepage** — Full-viewport atmospheric video intro — sells the destination, not the product.
2. **Three Lodge Pages** — Dedicated immersive pages for each property with rich storytelling layouts.
3. **Tours & Experiences** — Seasonal activity overview with maps and narrative copy.
4. **Multi-Step Inquiry Form** — GDPR-compliant. Captures inquiry type, guests, travel period, itinerary origin.
5. **Agent Portal** — Secure login for travel agents. Image bank and itinerary PDF downloads.
6. **AI Chatbot (Skilly)** — Conversational AI for site-wide search and high-intent lead capture.
7. **Bilingual Support** — English and Norwegian via TranslatePress. Scalable for future languages.
8. **Fully Responsive** — 320px to 1920px. WCAG Level A accessibility standards.
