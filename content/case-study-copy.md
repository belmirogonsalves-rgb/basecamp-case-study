## S02 — PROJECT OVERVIEW

**Section counter:** 01
**Section label:** Overview

**Intro (left column):**

Basecamp Explorer is Norway's leading Arctic adventure company — operating exclusive expeditions and premium accommodation in Svalbard, one of the world's most extreme and pristine environments. For over 25 years, they have been the definitive guide for high-value travellers seeking meaningful, off-the-beaten-path experiences in the High Arctic.

When they came to Kilowott, they had a clear ambition but a limiting digital presence. Their existing platform was technically failing, visually underwhelming, and almost entirely invisible to the international audience they needed most. The engagement covered a full website redesign and rebuild, alongside a structured SEO and GEO strategy designed to grow their global reach.

## S04 — THE PROBLEM: BUSINESS

**Section counter:** 03
**Section label:** The Challenge

**Headline:**
A World-Class Experience, Hidden Behind a Broken Website

**Subline:**
The gap between Basecamp Explorer's on-the-ground product and their online presence was significant. Four core business problems made a rebuild unavoidable.

**Problem cards (4):**

1. **Broken Booking Engine**
   The existing booking functionality was non-operational. High-intent visitors had no path to inquiry or conversion.

2. **Problematic Backend**
   The CMS was difficult to manage, making content updates slow and error-prone. The team could not keep the site current.

3. **UI Below Brand Standard**
   The visual design failed to communicate the premium, exclusive nature of Basecamp Explorer's product. The site looked ordinary. The experience was anything but.

4. **No Lead Generation Path**
   With no clear contact flow or call-to-action architecture, the site generated awareness but converted nothing.

## S07 — SOLUTION: WEBSITE

**Section counter:** 05
**Section label:** The Solution

**Headline:**
Building a Website Worthy of Svalbard

**Intro:**
The brief was clear: build something that sells the *feeling* of Svalbard, not a list of products. The new site needed to be immersive, lead-generating, and scalable — a platform that grows with the business.

**Phase timeline (4 steps):**

1. **Discovery** *(2–3 weeks)*
   Stakeholder interviews, platform audit, UX analysis, user journey mapping.

2. **Design** *(4–6 weeks)*
   Wireframes, interactive prototypes, high-fidelity UI. Every screen aligned to the premium brand.

3. **Development** *(8–10 weeks)*
   Custom WordPress build, ACF Pro, performance-optimised, responsive across all breakpoints.

4. **UAT & Deployment**
   Staging review, stakeholder sign-off, go-live, post-launch monitoring.

**Feature grid (8 features):**

1. **Video Hero Homepage** — Full-viewport atmospheric intro with Svalbard footage
2. **Three Lodge Pages** — Dedicated immersive pages for each property
3. **Tours & Experiences** — Seasonal activity overview with maps and narrative
4. **Multi-Step Inquiry Form** — GDPR-compliant, captures inquiry type, group size, period
5. **Agent Portal** — Secure login with image bank and itinerary downloads
6. **AI Chatbot** — Skilly integration for site-wide search and lead capture
7. **Bilingual Support** — English and Norwegian via TranslatePress
8. **Fully Responsive** — 320px to 1920px, all devices

**Tech stack labels:**
WordPress CMS · PHP · ACF Pro · HTML5/CSS3/JS · Formidable Forms · TranslatePress · Complianz · Skilly AI
